Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 05, 2026

Welcome to Akhil Bhartiya Kushwaha Mahasabha (ABKM). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by the ABKM application and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a community-building experience, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Identity: Full Name, Age, Gender, and Profile Image.
  • Contact Information: Mobile Number (used for authentication).
  • Community Context: Profession, Education, and Location (City, District, State, Tehsil, Village).
  • Social Interaction: Content of event applications and community activities.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Facilitating a community directory for members to connect.
  • Enabling event creation and attendance management.
  • Ensuring secure login via mobile-based authentication.
  • Improving the application features based on community feedback.

3. Data Deletion

We respect your "Right to be Forgotten". You can delete your entire profile and associated data directly through the Profile > Delete Profile button within the app. This action permanently removes your personal records from our databases.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (Supabase Row Level Security) to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for database management and image storage. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data handling.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at automation.sushil@gmail.com.
